The Problem

Critical Challenges in Indian Healthcare

India's healthcare system faces unique challenges that require innovative solutions tailored to its diverse population and geography.

Inaccessible Medical Records

Most hospitals use software only for admin work, while patients have no access to their medical records or history.

No AI Support

Zero AI assistance for reading, summarizing, or understanding documents, lab tests, or scan reports.

Time-Consuming Revisits

When patients return after weeks, doctors must manually re-read old records, wasting valuable consultation time.

Lack of Decision Support

Junior and training doctors lack adequate decision support during diagnosis and prescription.

Manual Analysis

No automation in analyzing reports—everything must be interpreted manually by healthcare providers.

Physical Record Risks

Many still carry physical files, and losing one means losing medical history entirely.
